ingredients:
- 10 cloves
- 2 cinnamon sticks
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 roughly grated nutmeg
- 2 cups of water
- cut up oranges
- 3 bottles of cheap red wine

directions:
- combine all ingredients in large pot
- Gently warm to low to medium heat (avoid boiling) for 20 minutes
- Serve with ladle into mugs
